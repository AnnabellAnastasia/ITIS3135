Apple

A website for a technology company that designs and sells consumer electronics, computer software, and online services.

  • Contrast
  • Repetition
  • Alignment
  • Proximity

Apple's website effectively utilizes the CRAP principles of design, with high contrast between text and background, consistent repetition of design elements, well-aligned text and images, and clear proximity between related content.
